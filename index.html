Isotope Analytical Services

Isotope Analytical Services is a premier metallurgical assaying laboratory located in Shurugwi, Zimbabwe. We specialize in precise testing for gold, platinum, lithium, and other minerals using state-of-the-art analytical techniques.

Our expertise primarily focuses on AAS (Atomic Absorption Spectroscopy) testing and Fire Assay methods, delivering accurate and reliable results for mining operations and exploration companies.
